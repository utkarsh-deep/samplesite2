The Living Mountain

As we pass the threshold of the winter solstice, the landscape in the Northen Hemisphere portrays a barren landscape so iconic for this time of year. These short days lead to long evenings and after cold walks on open hills, we long for open fires. The warmth and companionship of a great book feels essential to feed the soul and push back the darkness outside the walls of our homes.
